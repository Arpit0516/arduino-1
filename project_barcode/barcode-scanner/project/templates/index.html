About Us

This website was developed as part of our semester project under the guidance of Professor Dr Yogesh Wankhede. The goal was to create a real-time, efficient solution that saves users' time while shopping. Our platform allows for seamless and feasible shopping by incorporating an automatic QR scan feature, making the purchasing process faster and more convenient. Through this project, we aim to offer a smooth and user-friendly shopping experience that enhances convenience for all our customers.
